Why We're Different

Not Your Average Girls Trip

Built for you to become That Girl. Every detail of a TGE trip is designed for you to leave as a better, fuller, freer, more aligned & more connected version of yourself.

👯‍♀️
Supportive Community That Has Your Back
Our trips are curated to help you meet like-minded girls who are genuinely aligned, with connection-building moments seamlessly integrated into your days. The result? Deep, lifelong connections.
🐋
Bucket-list Experiences & Core Memories
Swimming with manta rays. Private liveaboards. Pink beaches at sunrise. We don't do ordinary. Every trip is built around bucket-list moments that become core memories.
🌟
Growth & Personal Development
Growth & Personal Development Workshops, Wellness Rituals, guided Vision-Board Making sessions to unlock "Next-Level You" & help you live your dream life. Real mindset & personal development - in paradise.

TGE-only Experiences
Experiences you won't find anywhere else. Ocean Ceremonies, Sunset Journalling, Pilates by the beach, Private liveaboards, boutique hotels & many more. We refuse to compromise on quality. No bunk beds, no dorms, no 8-person shared bathrooms. Ever.

Our signature Travel Identity™ quiz matches you with compatible roommates and future trip companions based on personality and preferences.

Safe Solo Travel, Sorted
Everything is taken care of - transfers, accommodation, activities, meals. You just show up. Travelling solo doesn't have to feel lonely or daunting anymore, and you get to meet new friends!
