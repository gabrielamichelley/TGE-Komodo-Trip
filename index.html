The Story Behind That Girl Experiences

I started That Girl Experiences because I wanted the kind of girls trip I always wished existed: for ocean lovers & adventurers, with a strong focus on wellness and growth, lifemaxxing together and genuinely transformative. Ever since I was young, I've always had a strong vision of helping people become the best version of themselves, living life to the fullest and building a supportive community of women who build each other up & want to see each other succeed - perhaps because this is what I've always wanted for myself. I genuinely believe that my true, core purpose is to build a supportive female travel community to help women see the world, become the best version of themselves and live our lives to the fullest together.

My story: I'm Indonesian — born, raised & lived there for 16 years before moving to Sydney, Australia. I've been to Bali more than 10+ times, I know the best places, the local & hidden spots, and I'll personally be hosting this trip with you from the start.
