FAQs

Everything you need to know before joining the trip.

Where do I fly into and fly out of?
You'll fly into and out of Denpasar (DPS). Once you land, we'll handle your airport pickup & take you to our villa! We'll also be going to 2 other islands (Nusa Lembongan & Nusa Penida), but we'll take care of everything for you! The trip will finish back in Bali (aka Denpasar (DPS)). We recommend booking your flights early for the best fares.
Who is this trip for?
Women aged 18–39 who love travel, growth & wellness (yoga/pilates/gym), want lifelong female friendships, want more out of life, a supportive girls community that builds you up & wants to see you succeed, and tick off bucket-list experiences. Whether you're coming solo or with a friend, we'd love for you to join us and help you live your dream life!
Do I need to be an experienced snorkeller?
Not at all! All snorkelling equipment is provided and our local guides will be with you the whole time. Basic swimming ability is recommended, but beginners are absolutely welcome — we'll make sure you feel comfortable and safe. You also don't need any prior scuba diving or snorkelling experience!
What is the Travel Identity™ matching system?
When you sign up, you'll take a fun travel quiz that uncovers your unique Travel Identity™. We use this to match you with roommates and future trip companions based on similar interests, personalities, and travel preferences — so you're paired with someone you'll genuinely click with.
Will I definitely see manta rays & sea turtles?
As they are wild animals, sightings cannot be 100% guaranteed, but we'll try our best to help you see them!
What's the room situation?
All rooms are twin-share with only 2 girls max per room (mix of twin beds & queen beds).
What if I have dietary requirements?
If you have any dietary requirements, please let us know as soon as you book so we can accommodate and prepare for it. We want to make sure everyone is well taken care of!
Wildlife sightings including manta rays, turtles, etc cannot be guaranteed as they are wild animals, but the likelihood is high & we'll try our best to help you see them!
