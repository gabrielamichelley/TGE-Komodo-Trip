FAQs

Everything you need to know before joining the trip.

Is TGE legit? How do I know my money is safe?
That Girl Experiences is a registered Australian business (ABN 44228347673) run by our founder, Gabs (more about her & her socials above!). Every booking is processed securely through WeTravel, the booking platform used by thousands of travel companies, with bank-level encryption via Stripe. WeTravel holds your payment and releases nothing to us until after your trip is completed. You'll also get a booking confirmation & receipt the moment you book, and you can message us or jump on a call before booking (no pressure/sales pitch, just answering your questions). We're new but we're real, and we've got nothing to hide🤍
How much is the trip all-in?
The total cost for the ISLAND LIFE Girls Trip is $599 USD if you come with a friend, or $699 USD if you come solo and includes everything in the itinerary and all the inclusions listed above. The deposit to secure your spot is $50 USD. You can pay the remainder in 4 payments/instalments at no additional costs before your trip departure via WeTravel.
Where do I fly into and fly out of?
You'll fly into and out of Denpasar (DPS), Bali. Once you land, we'll handle your airport pickup & take you to our accommodation! We'll also be going to 2 other islands (Nusa Lembongan & Nusa Penida), but we'll take care of everything for you! The trip will finish back in Bali - Denpasar (DPS). We recommend booking your flights early for the best fares.
Who is this trip for?
Women aged 18–39 who love travel, adventures, connect with like-minded girls, want to live life to its fullest & have a supportive girls community that builds you up - all while ticking off bucket-list experiences. Whether you're coming solo or with a friend, we'd love for you to join us and have the best time living your island life with the girls!
What kind of accommodation will we be staying at?
You'll be staying in a mix of private villa with a private pool & hotels (twin-share). No hostels or backpacker dorms ever.
What is the Travel Identity™ matching system?
Your unique Travel Identity™ showcases who you are when you travel, the way you travel, the experiences you crave, the energy you bring, and your travel DNA (what guides your travels). Your Travel Identity™ helps us tailor your trip experience, pair you with like-minded girls on your trip and match you with roommates and future trip companions based on similar interests, personalities, and travel preferences — so you're paired with someone you'll genuinely click with. Uncover your Travel Identity™ here.
Do I need to be an experienced snorkeller?
Not at all! All snorkelling equipment is provided and our local guide will be with you the whole time. Basic swimming ability is recommended, but beginners are absolutely welcome — we'll make sure you feel comfortable and safe. You also don't need any prior scuba diving or snorkelling experience!
What's the room situation?
We'll stay in a private pool villa and hotels. All the hotels will be twin-share with only 2 girls max per room (twin beds). No hostels or backpacker dorms ever.
Will I definitely see manta rays?
As they are wild animals, sightings cannot be 100% guaranteed, but we'll try our best to help you see them!
What if I have dietary requirements?
We can accommodate most dietary requirements. If you have any dietary requirements, please let us know as soon as you book so we can accommodate and prepare for it. We'd love for you to join us and we want to make sure everyone is well taken care of!
