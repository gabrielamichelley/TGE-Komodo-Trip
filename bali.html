FAQs

Everything you need to know before joining the trip.

How much is the trip all-in?
The total cost for the ISLAND LIFE Girls Trip is $499 USD ($699 AUD / £369) and includes everything in the itinerary and all the inclusions listed above. The deposit to secure your spot is only $50 USD / $70 AUD / £37. You can pay the remainder in 4 payments/instalments at no additional costs before your trip departure.
Where do I fly into and fly out of?
You'll fly into and out of Denpasar (DPS), Bali. Once you land, we'll handle your airport pickup & take you to our private villa! We'll also be going to 2 other islands (Nusa Lembongan & Nusa Penida), but we'll take care of everything for you! The trip will finish back in Bali (aka Denpasar (DPS)). We recommend booking your flights early for the best fares.
Who is this trip for?
Women aged 18–39 who love travel, adventures, connect with like-minded girls, want to live life to its fullest & have a supportive girls community that builds you up - all while ticking off bucket-list experiences. Whether you're coming solo or with a friend, we'd love for you to join us and help you live your dream life!
What kind of accommodation will we be staying at?
You'll be staying in luxe private villas with a private pool (2 girls max per room) & 4-star or above hotels (twin-share). No hostels, bunk beds or backpacker dorms ever.
What is the Travel Identity™ matching system?
Your unique Travel Identity™ showcases who you are when you travel, the way you travel, the experiences you crave, the energy you bring, and your travel DNA (what guides your travels). Your Travel Identity™ helps us tailor your trip experience, pair you with like-minded girls on your trip and match you with roommates and future trip companions based on similar interests, personalities, and travel preferences — so you're paired with someone you'll genuinely click with. Uncover your Travel Identity™️ here
Do I need to be an experienced snorkeller?
Not at all! All snorkelling equipment is provided and our local guides will be with you the whole time. Basic swimming ability is recommended, but beginners are absolutely welcome — we'll make sure you feel comfortable and safe. You also don't need any prior scuba diving or snorkelling experience!
What's the room situation?
All the accommodation will be twin-share with only 2 girls max per room (twin beds). We'll stay in both luxe private pool villas and 4-star or above hotels. No hostels, bunk beds or backpacker dorms ever.
Will I definitely see manta rays & sea turtles?
As they are wild animals, sightings cannot be 100% guaranteed, but we'll try our best to help you see them!
What if I have dietary requirements?
If you have any dietary requirements, please let us know as soon as you book so we can accommodate and prepare for it. We'd love for you to join us and we want to make sure everyone is well taken care of!
