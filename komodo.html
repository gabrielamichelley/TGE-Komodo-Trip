FAQs

Everything you need to know before joining the trip.

Who is this trip for?
Women aged 19–38 who love travel, growth & wellness (yoga/pilates/gym), want lifelong female friendships, want more out of life, a supportive girls community that builds you up & wants to see you succeed, and tick off bucket-list experiences. Whether you're coming solo or with a friend, we'd love for you to join us and help you live your dream life!
Do I need to be an experienced snorkeller?
Not at all! All snorkelling equipment is provided and our local guides will be with you the whole time. Basic swimming ability is recommended, but beginners are absolutely welcome — we'll make sure you feel comfortable and safe. You also don't need any prior scuba diving or snorkelling experience!
What is the Travel Identity™ matching system?
When you sign up, you'll take a fun travel quiz that uncovers your unique Travel Identity™. We use this to match you with roommates and future trip companions based on similar interests, personalities, and travel preferences — so you're paired with someone you'll genuinely click with.
Will I definitely see manta rays?
The chances are very high! The time of year we'll be going is considered a strong season in Komodo for manta ray sightings. However, as they are wild animals, sightings cannot be 100% guaranteed — but the odds are firmly in our favour.
How do I get to Labuan Bajo?
Fly into Labuan Bajo Airport (LBJ). There are direct flights from Bali (approximately 1 hour). We recommend booking your flights early for the best fares. Once you land, we'll handle your airport pickup.
What's the room situation?
All rooms are twin-share with only 2 girls per room (mix of twin beds & queen beds). At the luxe hotels on land, rooms will be twin-share with twin beds. On the liveaboard, each cabin has a queen bed with its own private en-suite bathroom, hot shower, and AC — 2 girls per cabin. Roommates are matched using our Travel Identity™ system so you'll be paired with someone you'll genuinely click with.
What if I have dietary requirements?
If you have any dietary requirements, please let us know as soon as you book so we can accommodate and prepare for it. We want to make sure everyone is well taken care of!
Wildlife sightings including manta rays and turtles cannot be guaranteed as they are wild animals, but the chances of success are very high and we'll be going during a 'strong likelihood' season in Komodo.
